To combine applied creativity with powerful online tools to accelerate growth for businesses

Media Red Jelly is a branding, web design and digital marketing agency with a specialization in online tools, automations and strategy.

Our proven Brand, Build, Grow process creates the online foundation that businesses need for lasting marketing success.

Why the Red Jellyfish?

Media Red Jelly was born in 2017 from a recognition that businesses needed a realistic and straightforward approach to growing effectively. We built our brand on the idea of working as efficiently as possible, much like the Media Red Jellyfish that inspired our name. The red jellyfish is the world’s most efficient animal and it continues to inspire us to be efficient, proactive and work hard for each and every one of our clients.

Media Red Jelly originally began as a full service digital marketing agency, helping businesses market themselves with the branding and website they already had. Over time, we realized that businesses often had little to no foundational branding and lackluster websites that weren’t converting into leads for their business, even after marketing. Media Red Jelly honed in on this need and pivoted into a unique niche that focused on three implementation steps for business growth: Brand, Build, and Grow.
